Business Rules:

- Each Vendor may provide multiple items to Inventory
- Inventory may receive multiple items from different Vendors

- Every Inventory Item has an Item Category
- An Item Category may be assigned to many Inventory Items

- Each Menu Item may contain many ingredients from Inventory
- Each Inventory Item may belong to many Menu Items

- Each Sandwich belongs to a Menu Item
- A Menu Item contains at least one sandwich type

- Each account transaction is for one Sandwich purchase
- A Sandwich may be purchased and accounted for multiple times

- Each Order contains at least one Order Line
- Each Order Line belongs to one Order

- A Customer may become a Loyal Customer
